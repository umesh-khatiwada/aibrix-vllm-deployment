# Logging In

## Accessing the Platform

Your organization's AMD Enterprise AI Suite is hosted at a URL provided by your administrator. Open it in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).

---

## Authentication Methods

### Single Sign-On (SSO)

If your organization has SSO configured, you'll be redirected to your company's identity provider (e.g., Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace).

1. Click **Sign in with SSO**
2. Enter your company email
3. Complete your organization's login flow (MFA if required)
4. You'll be redirected back to the platform

### Email Invitation

1. Check your inbox for an invitation from the platform
2. Click the link in the email
3. Set your password
4. Log in with your email and new password

### Manual Account

If your admin created an account for you directly:

1. Enter the email and temporary password provided
2. You'll be prompted to set a new password on first login

---

## After Logging In

Once authenticated, you'll land on the **main dashboard**. From here you can:

- Navigate to **AMD AI Workbench** for model work
- Navigate to **AMD Resource Manager** for infrastructure management
- Access your **Projects**
- Set up your [Personal Preferences](https://enterprise-ai.docs.amd.com/en/latest/personal-preferences.html)

---
